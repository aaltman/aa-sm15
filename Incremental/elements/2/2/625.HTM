: Pull-up

To avoid this HIGH impedance or floating state, a pull-up resistor is introduced. In this type, the internal pull-up resistor is connected to the pin. So, the state will be HIGH unless an external pull-down resistor is used. There will be a particular register to enable this pull-up resistor in the microcontroller.
