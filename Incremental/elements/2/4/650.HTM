: Mode 1

In this mode, CPOL is 0 and CPHA is also 1. So, the SCLK starts as Low and the data will be sampled at the second edge. Check the below image.

The first clock signal rising can be used to prepare the data. The clock idle state is LOW. The data on MISO and MOSI lines must be stable while the clock is low and can be changed when the clock is high. The data is captured on the clock’s high-to-low transition and propagated on low-to-high clock transition.
