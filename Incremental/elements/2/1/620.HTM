In the above image, T1 is the PMOS transistor and T2 is the NMOS transistor. When we write 1 from our code, the first inverter will make it as 0. So, the PMOS transistor (T1) will be activated and the NMOS transistor (T2) will be deactivated. Finally, T1 will take the Vcc and give it to the GPIO pin. If we connect the LED now, it will be ON.
