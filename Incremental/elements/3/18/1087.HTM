: Balance equations[edit]

The steady state equations for the birth-and-death process, known as the balance equations, are as follows. Here Pn denotes the steady state probability to be in state n.

μ1P1=λ0P0
λ0P0+μ2P2=(λ1+μ1)P1
λn−1Pn−1+μn+1Pn+1=(λn+μn)Pn

The first two equations imply

P1=λ0μ1P0

and

P2=λ1μ2P1+1μ2(μ1P1−λ0P0)=λ1μ2P1=λ1λ0μ2μ1P0.

By mathematical induction,

Pn=λn−1λn−2⋯λ0μnμn−1⋯μ1P0=P0∏i=0n−1λiμi+1.

The condition ∑n=0∞Pn=P0+P0∑n=1∞∏i=0n−1λiμi+1=1 leads to

P0=11+∑n=1∞∏i=0n−1λiμi+1

which, together with the equation for Pn (n≥1), fully describes the required steady state probabilities.
