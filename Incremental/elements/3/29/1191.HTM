: Bugs[edit]

Bryan Cantrill pointed out that epoll had mistakes that could have been avoided, had it learned from its predecessors: input/output completion ports, event ports (Solaris) and kqueue.[7] However, a large part of his criticism was addressed by epoll's EPOLLONESHOT and EPOLLEXCLUSIVE options. EPOLLONESHOT was added in version 2.6.2 of the Linux kernel mainline, released in February 2004. EPOLLEXCLUSIVE was added in version 4.5, released in March 2016.[8]
