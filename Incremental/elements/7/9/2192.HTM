: acks

This configuration specifies the required number of acknowledgments from the broker that the producer must receive to consider a message as successfully sent. There are three possible values:

  • 0: The producer does not wait for any acknowledgment. This setting provides the lowest latency and durability, as messages may be lost if a broker fails.
  • 1: The producer waits for the leader broker to acknowledge the message. This setting balances latency and durability, ensuring that messages are written to at least one replica.
  • 1 or all: The producer waits for all in-sync replicas to acknowledge the message. This setting provides the highest durability and latency, as the producer has to wait for all replicas to confirm the message.
